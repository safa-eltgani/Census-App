Welcome to the second stage of my life

I call this stage Growing Up:

  1. Moved to Cairo,Egypt:
    1. On July 2009,I woke up and hopped on a plane to foreign land, without saying goodbye to my old life. Did't know the chapter ended.
    2. I remember sand, and noise,Arabic. I felt excitement did know what loss lied ahead
  2. Went to the International School of Choueifat Egypt(2009-2018):
    1. From third grade to 12th grade, at this point I realised I was black, it didnt mean much as kid when I was in Austin, or not in a way I remember.
    2. I met all of my freinds here,the teachers would become family.
    3. Graduated with distinction top of my class.
  3. Went to the American University in Cairo (2018-2019):
    1. Did my Freshmen year there worked so hard to come to UW-madison.
    2. It was fun and horrible at the same time,met more people who have helped find myself but felt I lost the experince of growing in america
  4. Moved back to America(2019):

Interpretation:

In Egypt, I learned how to love, how to appreciate, how to live. I learned about racism,fears everything that has prepared for this moment. I learned about what I owe myself and society. I planned out what I needed and where I wanted to go.>
